Power Generation Structures

We design and fabricate turbine houses and power plant structures for gas and steam turbines. Our engineering team uses advanced structural analysis software including SAP2000 and ETABS to ensure structural integrity, seismic resistance, and compliance with international power plant standards.

We work with leading power companies to deliver reliable structural solutions for energy projects. Our ISO 9001:2015 certified processes ensure quality control throughout design, fabrication, surface treatment, and installation. All welding follows AWS D1.1 standards.

  • Turbine generator foundations and support structures
  • Complete power plant building structures
  • Heavy equipment support platforms
  • Exhaust stack supports and flue gas ducts
  • Control room and operation buildings
  • Transformer platforms and electrical substations
