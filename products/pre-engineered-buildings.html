Overview

Prefabricated Steel Structures

We provide complete pre-engineered metal building (PEB) solutions for rapid construction and cost-effective deployment. Our PEB systems use optimized structural design with tapered steel frames, standing seam roof panels, and insulated wall systems. Engineering design uses STAAD.Pro and PROKON software.

Our PEB solutions are completely customizable and designed for easy assembly with bolted connections. We provide turnkey solutions including structural frames, roofing and wall cladding systems, insulation, doors and windows, ventilation systems, and crane support beams. Delivery time is 50% faster than conventional steel buildings.

  • Warehouse and distribution centers (up to 60m span)
  • Manufacturing factories and production facilities
  • Automotive showrooms and retail buildings
  • Sports halls and recreation facilities
  • Agricultural storage and livestock buildings
  • Commercial offices and retail structures
