Overview

Bulk Storage Facilities

We fabricate precision-engineered storage tanks and silos for liquids, petroleum products, chemicals, grains, cement, and bulk materials. Our manufacturing process uses advanced plate processing equipment including ESAB SUPRAREX plasma cutting machines and ROCCA plate rolling machines.

Our tanks are designed according to international standards including API 650 for storage tanks, API 620 for low-pressure tanks, ASME Section VIII for pressure vessels, and AWWA D100 for water storage. All tanks undergo strict quality control including material traceability and hydrostatic testing.

  • Above-ground storage tanks (AST) up to 10,000m³ capacity
  • Vertical and horizontal grain and cement silos
  • Pressure vessels designed to ASME standards
  • Fuel and petroleum product storage tanks (API 650)
  • Potable and industrial water storage tanks
  • Custom plate fabrication work for specialized applications
