Overview

Bulk Storage Facilities

We fabricate precision-engineered storage tanks and silos for liquids, petroleum products, chemicals, grains, cement, and bulk materials.

Our tanks are designed according to international standards including API 650, API 620, and ASME Section VIII.

  • Above-ground storage tanks (AST) up to 10,000m³ capacity
  • Vertical and horizontal grain and cement silos
  • Pressure vessels designed to ASME standards
  • Fuel and petroleum product storage tanks (API 650)
  • Potable and industrial water storage tanks
  • Custom plate fabrication work for specialized applications
