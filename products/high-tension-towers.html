Overview

Electrical Transmission Systems

We fabricate high tension towers and transmission line structures for electricity distribution networks. Our towers are engineered using TELKA Structural and STAAD.Pro software for maximum durability, wind resistance, and compliance with international electrical transmission standards.

We use advanced detailing software including Autodesk Advance Steel and Tekla Structural to ensure precise fabrication, proper material traceability, and easy assembly on site. All towers are hot-dip galvanized for corrosion protection.

  • High voltage transmission line towers (up to 500kV)
  • Electrical substation steel structures
  • Switchyard gantries and support towers
  • Telecommunication and radio towers
  • Microwave antenna support structures
  • Distribution line poles and towers
