Overview

Transportation Infrastructure

Rawafid Alssalb Factory specializes in the design, fabrication, and installation of steel bridges and gantry structures for transportation infrastructure projects. Our engineering team uses state-of-the-art structural analysis software including CSI SAP2000, ETABS, and STAAD.Pro to perform load calculations, dynamic analysis, and seismic design.

We deliver high-quality bridge solutions that meet international standards including AASHTO bridge design specifications, AWS D1.1 and D1.5 welding codes for bridge construction, and ISO 9001:2015 quality management. All bridges undergo rigorous quality control with complete material traceability, NDT inspection, and comprehensive surface treatment.

  • Highway and railway bridge structures (spans up to 100m)
  • Pedestrian and cycling footbridge systems
  • Industrial overhead gantry crane structures
  • Complete structural load calculations and seismic analysis
  • Full surface treatment with shot blasting and protective coating
  • Professional delivery, site assembly, and installation services
