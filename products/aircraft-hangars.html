Overview

Aviation Maintenance & Storage Facilities

We design and fabricate large-span aircraft hangars for commercial airlines, military aviation, and private jet facilities. Our hangar structures provide clear spans up to 100 meters without internal columns, allowing unrestricted aircraft movement.

We provide complete hangar solutions including structural steel frames, heavy-duty hangar door systems, crane support beams, office and workshop areas, and auxiliary support buildings. All structures undergo complete surface treatment with shot blasting and protective painting systems.

  • Wide-span aircraft maintenance hangars (up to 100m clear span)
  • Climate-controlled storage hangars
  • Heavy-duty bi-fold and sliding hangar door systems
  • Runway and taxiway lighting support structures
  • Aviation workshop and office buildings
  • Fire suppression system support structures
