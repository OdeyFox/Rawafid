Overview

Freight Handling Facilities

We fabricate robust cargo terminal structures for airports, seaports, dry ports, and land border crossings. Our structures are designed to support efficient cargo handling operations with heavy-duty loading capacities, crane rail systems, and material handling equipment.

We deliver complete logistics infrastructure including large-span warehouse buildings, covered freight terminals, truck loading docks, conveyor bridge support structures, refrigerated cold storage facilities, and multi-level logistics platforms. All steel structures are fabricated with quality control procedures.

  • Large-span cargo warehouse buildings (up to 50m span)
  • Covered freight terminal structures with overhead cranes
  • Truck loading bay canopies and dock levelers
  • Conveyor belt support bridges and platforms
  • Temperature-controlled cold storage facilities
  • Multi-level logistics sorting platforms
