Heavy Industrial Structures

We fabricate and erect heavy-duty steel buildings for industrial facilities, manufacturing plants, warehouses, and commercial complexes requiring high load-bearing capacity.

We offer complete turnkey solutions including structural design, shop drawing preparation, precision fabrication, and on-site installation.

  • Hot rolled steel frame structures with heavy column sections
  • Multi-story industrial buildings (up to 5 floors)
  • Large-span warehouse structures (spans up to 50m)
  • Factory buildings with overhead crane support
  • Custom-engineered solutions for specialized industries
  • Complete civil works including foundation design
